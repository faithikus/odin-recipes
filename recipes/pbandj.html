How to Create a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Don't you want to create this wondrous sandwhich for your grandson?!?

Ok, so peanut butter was a success and now you want to build off of that?!?
Grandma Shirley has you covered. You can add JELLY to a peanut butter
sandwhich! How crazy is that?

You can add strawberry jelly. You can add peach jelly. You can add blueberry
jelly. You can add cherry jelly. You can add grape jelly. You can add blackberry
jelly. The possibilities are endless!

You could even add honey!!!

Ingredients

  • peanut butter
  • two slices ofbread
  • jelly of your choice

Steps

  1. Open the peanut butter
  2. spread a generous amount on one slice of bread
  3. spread a generous amount of jelly on the second slice of bread
  4. Place the jellied slice on top of the peanut buttered slice
  5. Wow! You did it
  6. cut it into triangles if you want to be the fun grandma
