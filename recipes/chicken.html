Delicious Chicken: Straight from the Fridge

Chicken is quite good for you. Very Delicious. That's why I keep offering it
to my grandson. If Ethan hopes to become a strong working man, he needs to eat!
I hope you understand the importance of a healthy diet

In this recipe I will show you how to always have chicken on hand, in your fridge
so that if anyone is hungry, you have just open the fridge, and hey presto! Chicken!!!!
Its almost as good as the nut hack I have. You know, keep a packet of nuts in your
purse so that you can break them out after the service ends for Yom Kippur. That
way you can break your fast and not wait on your crazy extended family to sit at the table

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt (kosher obviously)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh black peppeer
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Lay chicken breasts in a large baking dish, then brush on both sides with oil
  3. Mix seasonings together and sprinkle them evenly over chicken breasts
  4. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an interal temperature of 165F
  5. Let rest for 5-10 minutes
  6. Eat it now, or put it in the fridge and save it for your grandson!
