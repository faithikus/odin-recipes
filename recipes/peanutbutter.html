Peanut Butter Sandwhich

A Peanut Butter Sandwhich
Just the way Ethan likes it

Have you ever needed to feed a family member, but you down really want
to break out the frying pan? Look no further. In this recipe I will take you
through doing the bare minumum to put food on the table. The good news is that
kids like anything with sugar, and this has a lot of sugar!!

Your grandson will love it. He will grow into an amazing boy. Private schools
will offer him free admission. Even an idiot could make this sandwhich! An added
bonus is that if you add enough peanut butter it will glue his jaw shut, so
that he doesn't talk over your favorite TV show.

Ingredients

  • peanut butter
  • two slices ofbread

Steps

  1. Open the peanut butter
  2. spread a generous amount on one slice of bread
  3. Put the second piece bread directly on top of the peanut buttered slice
  4. Shake things up by cutting it into triangle. What a fun shape!
