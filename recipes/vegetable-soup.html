Vegetable Soup for your Grandson

Delicious Vegetable Soup

Ok so, your grandson is sick of your other recipes? Never fear! Canned soup
is here! Now you can offer endless options without having to hire a maid

Just make sure you stock up on every single soup option you can possibly
find the next time you go to the store. I suggest considering buying a house
that you can have more cabinet space to stash soup in. Sometimes my friends
ask me if I think I have enough soup, and I say hell no! How could I ever have
enough soup? Its canned! It can't go bad. It will nourish my beautiful
grandbabies

Ingredients

  • Can of Vegetable Soup
  • pot

Steps

  1. Open the can of soup
  2. Pour it in the pot
  3. Turn the stove on medium high heat and heat until soup is simmering
  4. put the pot in the freezer for 5 minutes (unless you want your grandson to have a burnt tongue!)
  5. Serve with crackers
